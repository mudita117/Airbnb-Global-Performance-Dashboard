# 🏡 Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard | Power BI Project

An interactive Power BI dashboard built to analyze Airbnb’s global performance across major cities.  
This project explores listings growth, pricing trends, market share, customer behavior, seasonality, host trust, and review patterns using powerful and interactive visualizations.

---

## 📌 Project Overview

The **Global Airbnb Performance Dashboard** provides a comprehensive analysis of Airbnb listings and customer engagement across multiple global cities.  
The goal of this dashboard is to transform raw Airbnb data into actionable business insights that support strategic decision-making.

This dashboard is designed for:
- Business & Data Analysts  
- Marketing Teams  
- Strategy & Growth Teams  
- Travel & Tourism Analysts  

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack

The dashboard was developed using:

- 📊 **Power BI Desktop** – Data visualization & dashboard creation  
- 🔄 **Power Query** – Data cleaning and transformation  
- 🧠 **DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)** – Calculated measures & KPIs  
- 🗂️ **Data Modeling** – Relationship building and cross-filtering  
- 📁 **File Format** – `.png` preview images  

---

## 📂 Dataset Information

Dataset: **Global Airbnb Listings and Review Dataset (https://mavenanalytics.io/data-playground/airbnb-listings-reviews)**

The dataset contains:
- Airbnb listings across major global cities  
- Host and property details  
- Pricing information by property type  
- Customer ratings and reviews  
- Review frequency and trust metrics  
- Seasonal travel trends  

---

# ⭐ Features & Highlights

---

## 📉 Business Problem

Airbnb operates globally and generates massive volumes of data.  
However, answering key questions quickly is difficult without a centralized dashboard:

- Which cities dominate Airbnb’s market share?  
- How has Airbnb grown over time?  
- What property types are most expensive?  
- How trustworthy are hosts?  
- How do customers behave when leaving reviews?  
- What seasonal travel patterns exist?

---

## 🎯 Goal of the Dashboard

To create a **single interactive analytics dashboard** that:

- Tracks Airbnb growth and listings trends  
- Analyzes pricing across property types  
- Understands customer behavior and reviews  
- Evaluates host trust and verification  
- Identifies seasonal travel demand

# 📊 Dashboard Walkthrough

---

# 🟢 Page 1 — Growth & Listings Overview

### 🔑 Key KPIs
- **279,712 Listings**
- **10 Cities Covered**
- **182,024 Hosts**
- **144 Property Types**
- **5.37M Reviews**

### 📈 Listings Growth Trend
This visual highlights Airbnb’s lifecycle:

- Rapid growth from **2010–2015**
- Peak maturity around **2015**
- Regulation slowdown during **2016–2017**
- Growth decline due to **COVID-19**

This helps understand how external factors impacted Airbnb supply.

---

# 🌍 Page 2 — Market Share, Pricing & Ratings

### 📊 Market Share by City (Pareto Analysis)
Key Insights:
- **Paris, New York & Sydney** contribute nearly half of total listings and reviews  
- **Paris** has the highest listings and reviews  

This helps identify high-demand cities.

---

### 💰 Average Price by Property Type

| Property Type | Avg Price |
|---|---|
| Hotel Room | $800 |
| Entire Place | $673 |
| Shared Room | $580 |
| Private Room | $462 |

This helps analyze Airbnb’s pricing strategy.

---

### ⭐ Ratings Analysis (Bookmark Navigation Feature)

This dashboard includes an **interactive bookmark toggle** to switch between two views:

#### ⭐ Overall Rating View
High-level comparison of city ratings.

#### 🔍 Detailed Rating View
Allows deeper exploration of rating metrics.

💡 **Why this is powerful**
- Demonstrates advanced Power BI skills (Bookmarks + Navigation)  
- Enables both executive summary & deep analysis in one page  
- Improves storytelling and user interactivity  

Top rated cities include:
- Mexico City  
- Rio de Janeiro  
- Cape Town  

---

# 📈 Page 3 — Customer Behavior, Reviews & Trust

### 📝 Review Frequency
Key findings:
- **98.8% of customers leave 3 reviews or fewer**
- Most users write only **one review**

This reflects realistic customer engagement.

---

### 🔒 Host Trust & Verification
- **66.9% hosts fully verified**
- Unverified hosts are extremely low  

Shows Airbnb’s strong trust ecosystem.

---

### 🌦️ Seasonality Trends
Key insight:
- **Paris & Rome dominate travel from April–August**
- Reflects peak European summer tourism.
